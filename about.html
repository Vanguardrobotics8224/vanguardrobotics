TEAM HISTORY

The Vanguard Robotics Team was founded by Vanguard High School in Montreal, Québec in 2016. Team members love to build, code, and compete against other teams. The Vanguard Robotics program introduces students to robots and technology while providing hands-on workshops for students with learning disabilities. The team first started by competing in the First Lego League competition. The Vanguard Robotics Team has won the Judges’ Award multiple times. In 2019, the team even reached fourth place in the First Robotics competition. In 2022, the robotics team competed in the FIRST Robotics Competition and won the prestigious Judges’ Choice Award for their dedication and perseverance throughout the year. In 2024 the team competed in FRC and CRC. In the 2025 season Vanguard Robotics once again the team competed in FRC and CRC (Canadian Robotics Competition). The team made it to the semi finals in CRC and did a great job at FRC. Currently the team has grown to almost 40 students and 10 mentors. This year we have made the decision to only compete in FRC. We will be attending the Festival de Robotique in Montreal, Quebec and the Canadian Pacific Regional in Vancouver for the team's first ever out of province competition. As a team we cannot wait to see the outcome for this season.

TEAM FUTURE

The team looks forward to now being a well-established FRC team, having purchased swerve drive and lots of other components that will be able to be reused. This will help us have a nice foundation for the years to come. In the next couple of years, we hope to get major sponsors and new room improvements. We eventually hope to have a larger team with great technologies and machines like CNC and milling machines. This year was a huge step for the team, as the room underwent a large facelift including new tools, parts, electrical systems as well as new organization systems. New mentors and teachers joined along to maximize our potential. It can only get better from here.

ABOUT VANGUARD

Who are we? Vanguard High School is a school designed to teach kids with learning difficulties. There is an English and French sector. Vanguard Elementary School was founded in 1973, the French sector of the high school was founded in 1987 and the English sector of the high school was founded in the mid-80s. The English sector became a real school in 1992 when it merged with the French sector. In 2013 all 3 of the Vanguard schools all moved onto one site, to where it is now. The school is located on Chemin de la Côte-de-Liesse in Saint-Laurent, Montréal.
