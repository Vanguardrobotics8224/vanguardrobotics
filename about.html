TEAM HISTORY

Vanguard Robotics is a team part of the Vanguard High School in Montreal, Quebec, who love to build, code, and compete against others. The Vanguard Robotics program introduces students to robots and technology while providing cross-curricular skill development enrichment for students living with learning disabilities. Vanguard Robotics was founded in 2016. The team first started by competing in the First Lego League competition. Vanguard Robotics has won the Judges’ Award multiple times. In 2019, the team even reached fourth place in the competition. In 2022, the high school senior robotics team competed in the FIRST Robotics Challenge and won the prestigious Judges’ Choice Award. In 2024 the team competed in FRC and CRC. In 2025 season Vanguard Robotics Team competed in FRC and CRC. The team made it to semi finals in CRC and did great at FRC. This year the team has grown to almost 40 students and 8 mentors. We have made the dicition to only compete in FRC, we will be attending the Festival de robotique in Montreal Quebec and Vancouver for the team first out of province competition. As a team we cannot wait to see the outcome of this season.

TEAM FUTURE

The team looks forward to now being a well established FRC team, having purchased swearve drive and lots of other components that will be able to be re-used. We will have a nice foundation for the years to come. In the next couple years we hope to get major sponsors, new room improvements, and plans for a new robotics room to be built in the schools new facilitys in comming years. We eventualy hope have a larger team with great technologies and machines like CNC and milling machines. THis year was a huge step for the team, with the room that got renevated and new tools, mentors, teachers and parts. It can only get better from here.

ABOUT VANGUARD

Who are we? Vanguard High School is a school designed to teach kids with learning difficulties. There is an English and French sector. Vanguard Elementary School was founded in 1973. The French sector of the high school was founded in 1987. The English sector of the high school was founded in the mid-80s. The English sector became a real school in 1992 when it merged in with the French sector. In 2013 all 3 of the Vanguard schools all moved onto one site, to where it is now. The school is located at 5935 Chemin de la Côte-de-Liesse in Saint-Laurent, Montreal.
